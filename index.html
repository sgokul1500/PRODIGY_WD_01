Our History

Brew Haven opened its doors in 1998 in the heart of Portland, Oregon. Founded by two lifelong friends, Alex Thompson and Jamie Miller, the coffee shop was born out of a shared passion for high-quality coffee and a love for community

Over the years, Brew Haven has garnered numerous accolades, including "Best Coffee Shop in Portland" by local publications and “Top 10 Coffee Shops in the U.S.” by a national magazine.

Here’s to many more years of shared moments over a cup of coffee." - Alex Thompson & Jamie Miller
